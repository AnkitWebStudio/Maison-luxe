The Art of
Bespoke

A commission from Maison Luxe is an invitation to ownership of something singular. Made entirely for you — by a single artisan, to your exact measurements, with your chosen materials — it is a piece that carries your story as much as our heritage.

How We Work

The Bespoke Process

01

Consultation

We begin with an unhurried conversation — in our atelier, by video, or by correspondence — to understand your vision, occasion, and preferences in material and silhouette.

02

Measurement & Selection

Your measurements are taken with obsessive precision — over 30 individual measurements for a garment. You will then select from our curated library of fabrics, sourced from the finest mills in England, Italy, and Japan.

03

The Making

Your piece is assigned to a single master artisan who will construct it from start to finish — over 60 hours for a tailored garment. Two fitting appointments are included to ensure perfect alignment.
