A Legacy of
Quiet Excellence

Founded on the principles of uncompromising craftsmanship and understated elegance, Maison Luxe has defined high-end minimalism for over a century.

— The Timeline —

1924

The Foundation

Established in Paris, focusing exclusively on bespoke leather goods for a discerning international clientele. The atelier quickly became synonymous with quiet refinement.

1958

The First Atelier

Expansion into ready-to-wear, maintaining the strict standards of bespoke tailoring. The opening of the Boulevard Saint-Germain atelier marked a new chapter in the house's story.

1981

An Icon is Recognised

The house receives its first international design award, cementing its reputation for architectural precision and material excellence across Europe and North America.

2010

Into the Digital Age

The house embraces a new generation of clients without compromising its founding philosophy. The objects collection launches — curated pieces designed to endure decades of use.

The Artisans

Masters of the Craft

Our artisans are the silent heartbeat of Maison Luxe. Many are second or third-generation craftsmen, dedicating their lives to the pursuit of perfection in every stitch and cut. They are not employees — they are the living embodiment of our ethos.
