The Signature
Cashmere Overcoat

An absolute classic, redefined. Meticulously tailored from 100% Mongolian cashmere, this overcoat offers unparalleled warmth with a weightless feel. The hand-felled seams, bespoke horn buttons, and structured shoulders are the hallmarks of a garment built to accompany you for decades.

The Signature Overcoat has been a cornerstone of the Maison Luxe offering since 1958, when it was first conceived for the opening of our Boulevard Saint-Germain atelier. Each coat is cut and assembled by a single master tailor over the course of three weeks, ensuring continuity of touch from the first snip of the shears to the final press.
Material: 100% Grade-A Mongolian Cashmere
Lining: Silk Twill
Buttons: Hand-polished Horn

Dry clean only. Store folded in the provided cedar-lined garment bag. Do not hang — cashmere stretches under its own weight. Use a cashmere comb annually to maintain the finish.
Shipping: Complimentary worldwide on all orders.
Delivery: 5–8 business days via DHL Express. Each piece is inspected and hand-wrapped before dispatch.

Returns: We accept returns within 14 days of delivery, provided the garment is unworn and in its original packaging. Bespoke and monogrammed pieces are non-returnable.
This garment is available as a fully bespoke commission — made to your exact measurements with your choice of cashmere weight (14oz or 18oz), lining fabric, button material, and monogram placement. Bespoke commissions take 8–12 weeks from consultation to delivery.
