A Legacy of Quiet Excellence

Founded on the principles of uncompromising craftsmanship and understated elegance, Maison Luxe has defined high-end minimalism for over a century.

The Timeline

1924

The Foundation

Established in Paris, focusing exclusively on bespoke leather goods for a discerning clientele.

1958

The First Atelier

Expansion into ready-to-wear, maintaining the strict standards of bespoke tailoring.
